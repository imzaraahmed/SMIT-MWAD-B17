Chicken Tandori Receipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 1kg
  • Yogurt - 1cup
  • Onions - 2
  • Ginger/Garlic paste - 1tbs
  • Oil - 1/4 cup
  • Piece of charcole
  • Seasoning:
    • Redpepper - 1tsp
    • Salt as per taste
    • cumin powder - 1tsp
    • Coriander powder - 1tsp
    • Warm spices - 1tsp

Instructions:

  1. At first, i am going to marinate the chicken, with yogurt, gingergarlic paste and all the spices mention in ingredients. And marinate it for 2 to 3 hours.

  2. Then finely chop the onions, meanwhile add 1/4 cup oil in a wok and fry the onions until they become brown.

  3. Afterthat, add the marination in onions and fry it for 3 to 4 minutes and add 1/2 cup water in it. Cook the chicken until it becomes tender and the water dry.

  4. At last put the heated charcole in the wok for smokey taste and garnish the chicken with coriander, green chillies and ginger.

  5. Serve it with hot naan and rice. chapati.
